How many colleges should list in my FAFSA form.
You should list at least one college, The schools you choose will use your FAFSA information to determine the types and amounts of aid you may receive.
Is there any particular order I should list my chosen schools.
For federal student aid, it doesn't matter in what order you list the schools, But for state aid, some states require that you list your schools in a specific order.
