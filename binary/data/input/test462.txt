What do federal regulations require.
Federal regulations require that all commercial learner permit holders or driver license CDL must certify to the type of commercial driving they perform right now or expect to perform; and USDOT Medical Examiner's Certificate will be required for some types of driving and must be submitted before current certifications expires.
Do I need to keep a USDOT Medical Examiners Certificate on file.
Yes, you must keep a current USDOT Medical Examiners Certificate on file with the DMV if you self-certify to a Non-Excepted Driving type NA or NI.
What happens if I have a commercial driver license, Class A,B,or C or CDL permit.
You must certify the type of driving you do anytime you perform a license transaction other than address change or duplicate license and be required to choose only one driving type.
