Tell me about VA claim exam, please.
Have you filed your claim for disability benefits.
No, I haven't.
Should I take a VA claim exam when filing a claim.
No, That's just if we need more information to decide your claim.
Will it be all right if I have enough medical evidence in my file to support my claim.
Yes, we won't ask you to have a claim exam, Medical evidence may include doctor and hospital reports, test results and other documents.
What's the next step after my exam.
After that, the doctor will write a report and send it to a VA claims processor to add to your claims file.
When can I do my VA claim exam.
The staff at your local VA medical center or local medical office that we partner with will contact you, They will mail you a letter with the date and time of your exam, or call you to find the best time.
