How do I get a licence to drive a taxi.
In New York State you must be 18 years of age and must have a class A CDL, Class B CDL, Class  C CDL or Class E driver license.
I am 18 years old,  How do I get a real ID.
Take required documents to the DMV.
Ok how do I get a Class E if I have a Class C.
You will complete the application, present your proof of identity and surrender your class C license and apply and pay for a Class E.
What is the fee.
The fee is based on the expiration date of your current driver license and we can not calculate that fee until you apply.
Do I have to take any test.
You have to take the vision test or prove that you meet our vision requirement.
Why do I need a REAL ID.
If you plan on traveling you will be required to have it.
Is a license required to become a taxi driver.
Yes, you must have a driver license that is either a Class A commercial driver license CDL, Class B CDL or one of two others.
If I don't get a real ID now will I have to later.
Yes, if you plan on traveling you will be required to have a REAL ID.
