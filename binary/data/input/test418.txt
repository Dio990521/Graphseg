How do I pay my driver civil penalty by mail.
Do you need to pay driver civil penalty by mail for uninsured operation or uninsured accident.
No it is not for uninsured operation or uninsured accident.
How do I know how much the penalty will be.
The reason for the suspension or revocation will determine the amount you pay.
Can I pay at the DMV office.
Yes, you are able to pay the driver civil penalty at the office listed on your suspension or revocation order; but is may faster and easier to pay online.
What is the amount I pay if the order was issued between June 1, 1972 and May 14, 2003.
The civil penalty would be $300 then.
What is the amount if the dates of the order was issued November 1, 1996 through October 31, 2006.
Then you will have to pay the civil penalty of $350.
What can I use to pay my penalty online.
You would be able to pay with a credit or debit card and your name and address on DMV records must be correct.
