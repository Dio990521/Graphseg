What are program reviews for federal student aid.
Do you want to know about its oversight duties.
Yes, what are the oversight duties of the program reviews.
FSA conducts program reviews to make sure a school meets FSA requirements for institutional eligibility, financial responsibility, and administrative capability.
What happens after completing a program review.
The FSA will issue a Final Program Review Determination which is just a report that has each finding identified in the program review report, the school's response, and Department's final determination.
What else would the FPRD do.
The FPRD will also identify liabilities, calculated based on the findings of the program review, provides instructions for the payment of those liabilities, notifies the institution of its right to appeal and closes the review.
